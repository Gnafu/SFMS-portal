Show/Hide a layer
Show/Hide a layer
Click on the group to expand the layer list
Show a layer
Click on the layer of interest and make sure it's blue
Hide a layer
Click on the layer of interest and make sure it's black
Layer opacity
1. When a layer is active, click on the icon to expand the available options
2. Adjust the slider to decrease (left) or increase (right) the layer opacity
Layer Legend
Layer Legend
Make sure the layer of interest is active (see show/hide layer)
Click on the icon to open the dashboard
Click on the legend button to see the legend

Forest stats
Click within the province of interest. When the system has loaded all data, click the ' Stats' button
Click on Việt Nam to see the national forest statistics
National/Provincial REDD+ profiles
National/Provincial REDD+ profiles

Click on Việt Nam to see the national REDD+ profile
Selection of time period
Selection of time period
