Title Emissions and removals matrices
Quantity 27 (one per ecoregion and country level for the period 195-2000, 2000-2005, 2005-2010)
Data owner VNFOREST
Data provider UN-REDD Programme (LOA FIPI-FAO No. FAOR 09/2015 and LOA FIPI-FAO No. FAOR 19/2016)
Data producer FIPI consigned under UN-REDD Programme (as instructed by MARD) based on VNFOREST owned data sets.
Data production date 2016 (as deliverable from the LOA FIPI-FAO No FAOR 19/2016, used for the Modified FRL submission to the UNFCCC)
Format and resolution tables based on the spreadsheet 13 to 15 of the Viet Nam Forest Reference (Emissions) Level calculations Excel file
Description of the data Table presenting the emissions (+) or removals (-) in tCO2 associated to each land cover change from one forest category to another for each 5 year period between two NFIMAP maps.
Remarks The emissions and removal matrices are obtained as the product of the area times the emissions and removals factors for each cell of the matrices.
